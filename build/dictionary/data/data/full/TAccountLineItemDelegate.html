TAccountLineItemDelegate (Virtual)
Description
The delegate for TAccountLineItem types. An entry of a specific amount of money, either crediting or debiting one T-account. A TAccountTransaction will contain a pair of balancing TAccountLineItems - a debit to one T-account and an equal credit to another.
