CustomPickerSearchResult (Virtual) (delegates to Versionable)
Description
Represents the results of a custom picker search. The custom picker mechanism is deprecated and is only provided for backwards compatibility. The extra configurability provided by PCF makes the entire custom picker mechanism redundant.
