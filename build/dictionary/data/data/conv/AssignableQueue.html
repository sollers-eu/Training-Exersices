AssignableQueue (cc_assignqueue) (delegates to Retireable)
Description
A queue of assignable entities. Entities can be assigned to a queue (rather than a user) and any entity assigned to a queue is said to be "in the queue". A queue belongs to a group and is typically used to organize items which can be handled by any user in its group. When a user has time to tackle a new item they look at the items in the queue and reassign the top item to themselves.
