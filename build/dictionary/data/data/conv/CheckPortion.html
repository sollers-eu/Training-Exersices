CheckPortion (cc_checkportion) (delegates to Extractable, Retireable)
Description
Indicates the amount of a multi-payee check that applies to a particular check. Checks with a CheckPortion do not have any payments, but just receive a percentage or fixed amount of the Payments in the primary Check of the CheckGroup (i.e., CheckGroup.PrimaryCheck).
